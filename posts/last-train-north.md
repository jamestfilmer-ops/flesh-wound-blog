---
title: "Ode to the Last Train North"
date: "2026-01-20"
category: "poem"
excerpt: "Written on a platform in February. The train was late."
readTime: "3 min read"
---

You arrive and I am already
half gone, coat on, bag at my feet,
which is how I love most things —
*ready to leave before they start.*

The platform empties around me.
A pigeon walks the yellow line
as if it knows the rule
does not apply to it.

I have not been north in three years.
What I am returning to
is not a place
*but the person I was when I left it.*

The train arrives on time.
I do not get on.
I stand there a moment longer,
watching it go.
